# PLACEHOLDER: Add your professional photo here

This file should be replaced with your professional photo.

**Requirements:**
- Filename: `profile.jpg` (or `profile.png`)
- Recommended size: 500x500px or larger (square aspect ratio)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Should be a professional headshot with good lighting

**How to add:**
1. Prepare your professional photo
2. Resize it to at least 500x500px (square)
3. Save it as `profile.jpg` or `profile.png`
4. Replace this file in the `/public` folder

The photo will appear in the hero section of your homepage.
